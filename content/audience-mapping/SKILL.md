# Audience Mapping

Know who you're writing for. Segment, score, and target.

---

## Context Questions

Before mapping audiences, ask:

1. **What's the topic/product?** — Broad topic, specific product, niche service
2. **What's your existing audience?** — None yet, small following, established base
3. **What's the content goal?** — Awareness, engagement, conversion, retention
4. **What platforms are you targeting?** — Blog, social, email, video
5. **What's your capacity?** — Single audience focus, multi-segment strategy

---

## Dimensions

| Dimension | Spectrum |
|-----------|----------|
| Segmentation | Broad/mass market ←→ Hyper-niche |
| Audience Size | Small/focused ←→ Large/diverse |
| Relationship | Cold/new ←→ Warm/existing |
| Content Style | One-size-fits-all ←→ Personalized per segment |
| Research Depth | Assumed personas ←→ Data-validated segments |

---

## Derivation Logic

| If Context Is... | Then Consider... |
|------------------|------------------|
| New product + no audience | Start broad, test angles, narrow based on response |
| B2B + specific industry | Deep persona work, pain points, decision-makers |
| Consumer + Gen Z | Platform-native, trend-aware, authenticity-first |
| Multi-platform + limited capacity | Primary audience focus, adapt format not message |
| High-value + low volume | Detailed persona, custom content, relationship-building |

---

## TL;DR

| Audience Type | Key Traits | Content Style |
|---------------|------------|---------------|
| **B2B** | ROI-focused, decision-makers | Data, case studies |
| **B2C** | Benefit-focused, emotional | Stories, how-to |
| **Gen Z** | Trend-aware, authentic | Short, visual |
| **Millennials** | Value + quality | Reviews, comparison |
| **Developers** | Technical, skeptical | Code, depth |
| **General** | Educational | Explainers |

## 1. Primary Audience Segments

### B2B (Business to Business)

```markdown
## B2B Audience Profile

### Who They Are
- Job titles: Manager, Director, VP, C-suite
- Industries: SaaS, Enterprise, Professional Services
- Goal: Solve business problems, improve metrics

### What They Care About
- ROI and cost savings
- Efficiency and productivity
- Risk reduction
- Competitive advantage
- Vendor credibility

### Content Preferences
| Format | Effectiveness |
|--------|--------------|
| Case studies | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| Whitepapers | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| Data/Reports | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| How-to guides | ⭐⭐⭐ |
| Short videos | ⭐⭐⭐ |

### Headline Hooks
- "How [Company] Achieved [Result]"
- "[X]% Increase in [Metric]"
- "The ROI of [Solution]"
- "[Industry] Leaders Are Doing This"
```

### B2C (Business to Consumer)

```markdown
## B2C Audience Profile

### Who They Are
- Demographics: Varies by product
- Goal: Solve personal problems, improve life

### What They Care About
- Benefits (not features)
- Price and value
- Social proof (reviews)
- Convenience
- Emotional connection

### Content Preferences
| Format | Effectiveness |
|--------|--------------|
| How-to guides | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| Product reviews | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| Comparison posts | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| Video tutorials | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| Listicles | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |

### Headline Hooks
- "How to [Achieve Desire] Without [Pain]"
- "[X] Best [Products] for [Need]"
- "I Tried [Product] for [Time]. Here's What Happened"
- "The [Adjective] Guide to [Topic]"
```

### Gen Z (1997-2012)

```markdown
## Gen Z Audience Profile

### Who They Are
- Age: 13-28 (as of 2025)
- Platforms: TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Discord
- Attention span: Short (but will engage if hooked)

### What They Care About
- Authenticity (no corporate speak)
- Social causes
- Trends and cultural relevance
- Entertainment + education
- Community belonging

### Content Preferences
| Format | Effectiveness |
|--------|--------------|
| Short-form video | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| Carousels | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| Memes | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| User-generated | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| Long-form blog | ⭐⭐ |

### Content Rules
- Hook in first 1 second
- Native to platform (don't repurpose lazily)
- Show don't tell
- Raw > polished
- Comments matter (engage back)

### Headline Hooks
- "TikTok Made Me Buy This"
- "POV: You [Relatable Situation]"
- "Why Nobody Talks About [Topic]"
- "[Trend] Changed Everything"
```

### What They Care About
- Does it actually work?
- Performance and scalability

- Documentation

### Content Preferences
| Format | Effectiveness |
|--------|--------------|

## 2. Interest Scoring

### Scoring Matrix

| Signal | Score |
|--------|-------|
| Topic directly solves their problem | +3 |
| Topic relates to their industry | +2 |
| Topic is trending in their space | +2 |
| Topic requires prior knowledge they have | +1 |
| Topic is general interest | +1 |
| Topic is unrelated to their context | 0 |
| Topic conflicts with their beliefs | -1 |

### Output Example

```markdown
## Interest Score: AI Image Generation

| Audience | Score | Reasoning |
|----------|-------|-----------|
| B2B (Marketing) | 5/5 | Solves design cost problem |
| B2C (Creators) | 4/5 | Solves content need |
| Gen Z | 3/5 | Trendy but not urgent |
| Developers | 4/5 | API integration interest |
| General | 2/5 | Awareness but no urgency |
```

## 3. Angle Recommendations

### Angle Template

```markdown
## Topic: [TOPIC]

### Audience: B2B
- **Angle:** [Value prop for business]
- **Hook:** [Headline or opening]
- **Format:** [Content type]
- **CTA:** [Action you want]

### Audience: B2C
- **Angle:** [Value prop for consumer]
- **Hook:** [Headline or opening]
- **Format:** [Content type]
- **CTA:** [Action you want]

### Audience: Gen Z
- **Angle:** [Trend/relevance angle]
- **Hook:** [Headline or opening]
- **Format:** [Content type]
- **CTA:** [Action you want]
```

### Angle Examples

```markdown
## Topic: Notion Templates

### B2B (Teams)
- **Angle:** "Reduce project chaos with these Notion systems"
- **Hook:** "Our team saved 10 hours/week with this setup"
- **Format:** Case study + template download
- **CTA:** Download template pack

### B2C (Productivity enthusiasts)
- **Angle:** "Organize your entire life in Notion"
- **Hook:** "The only Notion setup you need"
- **Format:** YouTube tutorial + blog
- **CTA:** Subscribe + download

### Gen Z (Students)
- **Angle:** "Aesthetic Notion templates that actually work"
- **Hook:** "My viral Notion setup (free template)"
- **Format:** TikTok + carousel
- **CTA:** Link in bio
```
