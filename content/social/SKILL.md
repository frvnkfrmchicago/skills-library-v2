# Social Content Strategy

Plan content that actually gets engagement.

> **See also:** Platform-specific skills for TikTok, Instagram, YouTube

---

## Context Questions

Before planning content, ask:

1. **Which platforms are you focusing on?** — TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn, Twitter/X
2. **What's your content capacity?** — Daily, 3x/week, weekly batching
3. **What's your primary goal?** — Followers, engagement, leads, sales, community
4. **What's your niche/topic?** — Broad (lifestyle) vs narrow (SaaS for dentists)
5. **Do you have a content team?** — Solo vs team vs agency

---

## Dimensions

| Dimension | Spectrum |
|-----------|----------|
| Platforms | Single-platform focus ←→ Multi-platform presence |
| Frequency | Quality (few posts) ←→ Volume (daily+) |
| Content Type | Evergreen ←→ Trending/reactive |
| Production | Lo-fi/authentic ←→ Polished/branded |
| Repurposing | Unique per platform ←→ Cross-post everything |

---

## Derivation Logic

| If Context Is... | Then Consider... |
|------------------|------------------|
| Solo creator + low capacity | Pick 1-2 platforms, batch content, repurpose heavily |
| Brand + team | Multi-platform, unique content per platform, calendar |
| Growth priority + new account | Volume approach, trending content, engagement focus |
| Lead gen + B2B | LinkedIn primary, educational content, DM strategy |
| Entertainment + personality | TikTok/Reels primary, trend-reactive, daily posting |

### Batching Strategy

```markdown
## Content Batching

Day 1: Ideation (30 min)
- Brainstorm 20 content ideas
- Check trending topics
- Review analytics

Day 2: Creation (2-3 hours)
- Write all captions
- Create graphics/videos
- Edit and polish

Day 3: Scheduling (30 min)
- Schedule week's content
- Set reminders for engagement
- Prep Stories/ephemeral content
```

## Part 2: Caption Formulas

### The Hook Formula

```markdown
## Hook Types

**Question Hook**
"Want to know why 90% of startups fail?"

**Controversial Hook**
"Unpopular opinion: [statement]"

**Story Hook**
"I lost $50K because I didn't know this..."

**Number Hook**
"7 tools that changed my workflow"

**Direct Hook**
"Stop scrolling. This will save you hours."
```

### PAS Formula (Problem-Agitate-Solve)

```markdown
## PAS Caption

**Problem:** "Spending 5+ hours on social media with nothing to show?"

**Agitate:** "Meanwhile, your competitors are getting leads on autopilot. Every day you wait is a day they're getting ahead."

**Solve:** "Here's the 3-step system I use to create a week's content in 2 hours. 👇"

[Content]

[CTA]
```

### AIDA Formula (Attention-Interest-Desire-Action)

```markdown
## AIDA Caption

**Attention:** "I made $10K last month from one skill."

**Interest:** "It's not coding. Not design. Not something that takes years to learn."

**Desire:** "Imagine waking up to notifications of money hitting your account while you sleep."

**Action:** "Comment 'GUIDE' and I'll DM you the free playbook."
```

[Topic] explained in 60 seconds:

3/ [Point three]

Like + RT if this helped."

## Part 3: Hashtag Strategy

### Hashtag Research

```markdown
## Finding Hashtags

1. **Competitor Analysis**
   - What hashtags do top creators use?
   - Which posts perform best?

2. **Platform Search**
   - Search your keyword
   - Note related hashtags
   - Check post volume

3. **Tools**
   - Hashtagify
   - RiteTag
   - Display Purposes
   - Platform native suggestions
```

### Hashtag Mix

```markdown
## The 3-Tier System

**Big (1M+ posts):** 2-3 hashtags
- #entrepreneur #startup #business

**Medium (100K-1M posts):** 3-5 hashtags
- #startuptips #founderlife #buildingpublic

**Small (<100K posts):** 5-10 hashtags
- #solofounder #indiehacker #shipfast

## Platform Limits
- Instagram: 30 max (use 15-20)
- Twitter/X: 2-3 max
- TikTok: 3-5 in caption
- LinkedIn: 3-5 max
```

### Hashtag Organization

```markdown
## Save Hashtag Sets

**Set 1: Startup/Tech**
#startup #founder #tech #saas #buildinpublic #indiehacker

**Set 2: Personal Brand**
#personalbrand #thoughtleader #contentcreator #linkedin

**Set 3: AI/Tech**
#ai #artificialintelligence #machinelearning #gpt #automation

Rotate sets to avoid shadow banning.
```

## Part 4: Engagement Hooks

### First Line Patterns

```markdown
## Stop-the-Scroll Openers

1. "You're doing [X] wrong."
2. "The truth about [topic] nobody talks about:"
3. "I wish I knew this when I started..."
4. "[Big result] in [short time] — here's how:"
5. "Delete this if you want, but..."
6. "This post will make some people angry."
7. "Forget everything you know about [topic]."
8. "My [role] just asked me about [topic]. Here's what I said:"
9. "Hot take: [controversial opinion]"
10. "POV: You just discovered [thing]"
```

### Carousel Hooks

```markdown
## Slide 1 (Cover)

"[Number] [Things] That Will [Result]"
"How I [Achievement] (Step-by-Step)"
"Stop [Bad Thing] — Do This Instead"
"[Year] Guide to [Topic]"

## Pattern
- Slide 1: Hook + promise
- Slides 2-8: Value delivery
- Final slide: CTA + summary
```

## Part 6: Cross-Platform Repurposing

### Content Matrix

```markdown
## One Idea → Multiple Posts

**Original:** Blog post (1500 words)

Repurpose to:
1. Twitter thread (10 tweets)
2. LinkedIn post (condensed)
3. Instagram carousel (8 slides)
4. TikTok video (60 sec summary)
5. YouTube Short (60 sec)
6. Email newsletter
7. Podcast talking points

## Effort Ratio
- Create once: 3 hours
- Repurpose 7 ways: 1 hour
- Total reach: 10x
```

### Format Conversions

```markdown
## Conversion Patterns

**Long → Short**
- Blog → Twitter thread → Individual tweets
- YouTube video → Shorts → TikTok → Reels

**Visual → Text**
- Infographic → Twitter thread
- Video → Blog transcript → Article

**Text → Visual**
- Tweet → Quote graphic
- Blog points → Carousel slides
- Stats → Infographic
```
