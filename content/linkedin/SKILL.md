# LinkedIn Content

Build thought leadership and generate leads on LinkedIn.

---

## Context Questions

Before creating content, ask:

1. **What's your goal?** — Thought leadership, lead gen, hiring, brand awareness, community
2. **Who's your target audience?** — Job titles, industries, company sizes, pain points
3. **What's your unique angle?** — Your story, expertise, contrarian take
4. **What's your capacity?** — Daily posting? 3x/week? Newsletter only?
5. **Personal brand or company brand?** — Human voice vs corporate voice

## 1. Algorithm 2025-2026

### How It Works

LinkedIn prioritizes content based on:

| Signal | Weight | What It Means |
|--------|--------|---------------|
| **Dwell Time** | High | How long people read your post |
| **Comments** | High | Real conversations (not just "Great!") |
| **Early Engagement** | High | First 60 minutes critical |
| **Reactions** | Medium | Likes, celebrates, etc. |
| **Saves** | Medium | Bookmarked for later |
| **Shares** | Medium | Within LinkedIn only |
| **External Clicks** | Low | Links out = penalty |
| **Hides/Reports** | Negative | Kills reach immediately |

### Key Insights

```
✅ What Works:
- Native content (no external links in main post)
- Text posts (still highest reach)
- Document carousels (high engagement)
- Comments from you within first hour
- Tagging relevant people (sparingly)

❌ What Hurts:
- External links in post body
- Engagement pods (detected and penalized)
- Hashtag stuffing
- Posting and disappearing
- Same content across multiple accounts
```

### Creator Mode

Now default for all accounts:
- Follower focus (Follow button prominent)
- Profile shows content first
- Featured section more visible
- Newsletter unlocked

## 2. Post Formats

### Text Posts (Highest Reach)

**Optimal Structure:**
```
[HOOK: Bold statement or question]

[Space]

[Personal story or context - 2-3 sentences]

• Point 1
• Point 2  
• Point 3

[Space]

[Key takeaway - 1 sentence]

[CTA: Question to drive comments]
```

**Formatting Tips:**
- 1,300-2,000 characters sweet spot
- First 2 lines = hook (before "see more")
- Line breaks every 1-2 sentences
- Emojis: 1-3 max, not every line
- Bullets: • or → for lists

### Document Posts (PDF Carousels)

**High engagement format for educational content:**

| Slide | Content |
|-------|---------|
| 1 | Hook + title (make them swipe) |
| 2-8 | One point per slide (7-10 slides total) |
| 9 | Summary or key takeaway |
| 10 | CTA (follow, comment, download) |

**Design Tips:**
- Large readable text (people scroll fast)
- Consistent brand colors
- Your face on slide 1 or 10
- Save as PDF (not images)

### Video (Growing)

**Native upload only** — YouTube links get buried.

| Spec | Recommendation |
|------|----------------|
| Length | 30-90 seconds |
| Format | Vertical (9:16) or Square (1:1) |
| Captions | Required (80% watch muted) |
| Style | Talking head + text overlays |

### Newsletters

The most underrated LinkedIn feature:

- Every subscriber gets notified (email + push)
- Published weekly = maximum consistency
- SEO indexed by Google
- Build subscriber base separately from followers

**Newsletter vs Article:**
- Newsletter: Subscribers get notified ✅
- Article: No notification, less reach ❌

## 3. Content Strategy

### Content Pillars for B2B

| Pillar | Example Topics |
|--------|----------------|
| **Industry Insights** | Trends, market analysis, predictions |
| **Personal Journey** | Lessons learned, failures, wins |
| **How-To** | Tutorials, frameworks, processes |
| **Contrarian Takes** | Challenge conventional wisdom |
| **Behind-the-Scenes** | Company culture, building in public |

### Posting Frequency

```
Starting out: 3-5x per week
Building presence: Daily (Mon-Fri)
Established: 3x minimum, daily optimal

Consistency > Frequency
Same time each day builds habit
```

### Best Posting Times (EST)

| Time | Why |
|------|-----|
| 7-9 AM | Pre-work scrolling |
| 12-1 PM | Lunch break |
| 5-6 PM | End of work day |

**Avoid:** Weekends (40% less engagement)

## 4. Hook Patterns

### Types That Work

**Bold Statement:**
```
I turned down a $200K job offer.

Here's why it was the best decision of my career:
```

**Question Hook:**
```
Why do 90% of startups fail in the first year?

(Hint: It's not what you think)
```

**Contrarian Take:**
```
Unpopular opinion: Hustle culture is killing innovation.

Let me explain:
```

**Story Hook:**
```
Last Tuesday, I got a call that changed everything.

A Fortune 500 CEO wanted to talk...
```

**Data Hook:**
```
We analyzed 10,000 LinkedIn posts.

Here's what the top 1% do differently:
```

## 5. Engagement Tactics

### First Hour Critical

```
Post goes live → Stay active for 60 minutes

1. Respond to EVERY comment within 5 min
2. Ask follow-up questions
3. Tag people who would find it valuable
4. Add a "bonus" comment with extra value
```

### Comment Strategy

**On Your Posts:**
- Never leave a comment unanswered
- Ask questions to extend conversations
- Pin your best comment

**On Others' Posts:**
- Comment on bigger accounts = visibility
- Thoughtful comments > generic praise
- Early comments get more visibility

**Good Comment Examples:**
```
✅ "This resonates. We tried X and found Y worked better because..."
✅ "Curious about your take on [specific aspect]?"
✅ "Adding to this: another pattern I've seen is..."

❌ "Great post!"
❌ "👏👏👏"
❌ "Interesting"
```

### DM Strategy

```
Warm Outreach Framework:
1. Engage with their content for 2-4 weeks
2. Send genuine, personalized DM
3. No pitch in first message
4. Offer value first

Voice messages stand out (3% of people use them)
```

## 6. Lead Generation

### Profile Optimization

| Element | Optimization |
|---------|--------------|
| **Headline** | Value proposition, not job title |
| **Banner** | CTA with offer/benefit |
| **About** | Story → Problem you solve → CTA |
| **Featured** | Lead magnets, best content |
| **Experience** | Results, not responsibilities |

**Headline Formula:**
```
I help [AUDIENCE] achieve [RESULT] through [METHOD]

Examples:
"I help B2B SaaS founders close enterprise deals 2x faster"
"Helping product teams ship faster with AI-powered workflows"
```

### Content-to-Lead Funnel

```
Content → Profile Visit → Follow → DM/Newsletter → Lead

Conversion points:
- Newsletter signup CTA in posts
- Lead magnet in Featured section
- Calendly in About section
- DM automation (carefully)
```

### Lead Magnets

What works on LinkedIn:
- PDF guides (document posts as teaser)
- Free templates
- Checklists
- Mini courses
- Newsletter exclusive content

## 7. LinkedIn Live & Audio

### LinkedIn Live

**Requirements:**
- 150+ followers OR creator mode enabled
- Clean account history

**Tools:**
- StreamYard, Restream, Ecamm for streaming
- Schedule 24-48 hours ahead
- Promote in posts before going live

**Best Practices:**
- Engage with comments live
- Interview format performs well
- Repurpose as video clips after

### Audio Events

LinkedIn's audio rooms:
- Lower bar than Live (easier to start)
- Great for discussions
- Build authority as host
