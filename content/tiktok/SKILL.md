## Context Questions

Before creating content, ask:

1. **What's your niche?** — Topic area, who you serve, what value you provide
2. **What's your personality on camera?** — High energy, calm expert, comedic, relatable
3. **Trend-chaser or original content?** — Speed vs consistency trade-off
4. **What's your goal?** — Virality, community, product sales, brand awareness
5. **What's your posting capacity?** — 1x/day minimum recommended, can you sustain?

|------------------|------------------|

## Part 1: The First 3 Seconds

### Hook Patterns

```markdown
## Visual Hooks (No Speaking)

1. **Action Start** — Begin mid-action, no intro
2. **Text Overlay** — "Wait for it..." or question
3. **Pattern Interrupt** — Unexpected visual
4. **Face Close-Up** — Eye contact with camera
5. **Movement** — Walking towards camera

## Spoken Hooks

1. "Stop scrolling if you..."
2. "POV: [scenario]"
3. "This is your sign to..."
4. "I need to tell you something..."
5. "Nobody talks about this but..."
6. "The one thing that changed everything..."
7. "You're doing it wrong. Here's why..."
8. "3 things I wish I knew..."
9. "[Controversial statement]"
10. "Replying to @[commenter]..."
```

### What Kills Hooks

```markdown
## Don't Do This

❌ "Hey guys, welcome back to my channel..."
❌ Long intro explaining what you'll cover
❌ Asking them to like/follow first
❌ Slow movement or static opening
❌ Low energy or boring tone
❌ Waiting to show the payoff
```

## Part 2: Trending Sounds

### Finding Trending Audio

```markdown
## Discovery Methods

1. **Scroll FYP** — Note sounds on viral videos
2. **Search Tab** — Check "Trending" sounds
3. **Discover Page** — Featured sounds section
4. **Creator Search** — Filter by recent, note sounds
5. **External Tools**
   - TokBoard (tokboard.com)
   - TrendTok
   - Tokcount

## Sound Categories
- Original audio → Your voice/sound
- Trending music → Lip sync, dance
- Trending voiceovers → Overlay your content
- Viral sounds → Remix/duet potential
```

### Using Sounds Strategically

```markdown
## Best Practices

1. Use trending sounds within 24-48 hours of trending
2. Match sound mood to your content tone
3. Use sound even if muted (algorithm still recognizes)
4. Create original sounds for brand recognition
5. Save sounds for later when you discover them

## Sound-First Workflow
1. Find trending sound
2. Think of content that fits
3. Create within 24 hours
4. Post at peak time
```

## Part 3: TikTok Hashtags

### Hashtag Strategy

```markdown
## The Formula

3-5 hashtags total:

1. **Niche Tag** (#startuptok, #techtok)
2. **Trend Tag** (#fyp, #viral)
3. **Topic Tag** (#[your topic])
4. **Challenge Tag** (if applicable)
5. **Sound Tag** (if applicable)

## Example
#techtok #entrepreneur #ai #fyp #buildingpublic
```

### Hashtag Research

```markdown
## Finding Tags

1. Search your topic → See related hashtags
2. Check competitor posts
3. Note hashtags on viral videos in your niche
4. Use TikTok's autocomplete
5. Rotate hashtags to test what works
```

## Part 4: FYP Algorithm

### How It Works (2025)

```markdown
## Algorithm Signals

**Positive Signals:**
- Watch time (most important)
- Rewatches
- Shares
- Comments
- Follows from video
- Profile visits

**Negative Signals:**
- Skip past your video
- "Not Interested" clicks
- Low completion rate

## The Feedback Loop
1. Shows video to small test group (~500)
2. Measures engagement metrics
3. Good metrics → Larger audience
4. Repeat until engagement drops
```

### Optimizing for FYP

```markdown
## Tactics

1. **Front-load value** — Best content in first 3 sec
2. **Keep it short** — 15-30 sec performs best initially
3. **Loop potential** — End leads back to beginning
4. **Text hooks** — Add captions for retention
5. **Reply to comments** — Algorithm loves creator engagement
6. **Post consistently** — 1-3x/day ideal
7. **Engage with niche** — Comment on others' content
```

## Part 6: Video Types

### Content Formats

```markdown
## High-Performing Formats

1. **POV Videos**
   "POV: You're a dev who just discovered..."

2. **Tutorial/How-To**
   "How to [result] in [timeframe]"

3. **Day in the Life**
   "A day in my life as a [role]"

4. **Storytime**
   "That time I [dramatic story]..."

5. **Trend Remixes**
   Take trending format + your niche

6. **Duets/Stitches**
   React to others' content

7. **Before/After**
   Transformation content

8. **Ranking/Tier Lists**
   "Ranking [things] from worst to best"
```

### Length Optimization

```markdown
## Video Length

**7-15 seconds:** Quick tips, hooks, memes
**15-30 seconds:** Tutorials, storytime teasers
**30-60 seconds:** Detailed how-tos, stories
**1-3 minutes:** Deep dives, storytimes
**3+ minutes:** Series content (harder to viral)

## Rule
Shorter = higher completion rate = more FYP push
Only go long if content demands it
```

## Part 7: Duets and Stitches

### Stitch Strategy

```markdown
## How to Stitch

1. Find viral video with discussion potential
2. Tap Share → Stitch
3. Record your reaction/addition (up to 5 sec of original)
4. Add your value/take

## Stitch Ideas
- "I disagree, here's why..."
- "Adding to this..."
- "Here's the full story..."
- "Let me explain..."
```

### Duet Strategy

```markdown
## Duet Types

1. **Reaction Duet**
   React in real-time to content

2. **Add-On Duet**
   Your content plays alongside

3. **Tutorial Duet**
   Show how to do what they're doing

## Finding Duet Opportunities
- Videos asking for responses
- Controversial takes
- Tutorials you can add to
- Challenges
```

## Part 9: Caption Overlays

```markdown
## Text Best Practices

1. **Large, readable font** — Mobile-first
2. **Contrasting colors** — White text, black outline
3. **Position safely** — Avoid bottom (buttons) and top (handle)
4. **Sync with speech** — Auto-caption tools help
5. **Add hook text** — First frame grabs attention

## Caption Styles
- Subtitle style (spoken word)
- Highlight style (key words pop)
- Full screen text (no speaking)
- Emoji-enhanced
```
