# Instagram Content

Master Reels, carousels, and Stories.

> **See also:** `content/social/SKILL.md` for general strategy

---

## Context Questions

Before creating content, ask:

1. **Who is your audience?** — Age, interests, pain points, what they're scrolling for
2. **What's your brand voice?** — Professional, casual, edgy, educational, entertaining
3. **What's your goal?** — Followers, engagement, sales, community, authority
4. **What's your capacity?** — Daily posting? Weekly? What's sustainable?
5. **What's your content type?** — Face-on-camera, faceless, product, lifestyle, educational

---

## Dimensions

| Dimension | Spectrum |
|-----------|----------|
| Tone | Professional ←→ Casual/Playful |
| Production | Raw/Authentic ←→ Polished/Edited |
| Frequency | Quality-focused (2-3/week) ←→ Volume (daily+) |
| Format Mix | Single format specialist ←→ Multi-format creator |
| Engagement | Broadcast (post only) ←→ Community (high interaction) |

---

## Derivation Logic

| If Context Is... | Then Consider... |
|------------------|------------------|
| B2B audience + authority goal | Carousels heavy, educational tone, professional aesthetic |
| Gen Z + entertainment goal | Reels heavy, trending sounds, raw production |
| Product sales + low capacity | Stories for sales, carousels for education, 3x/week |
| Personal brand + face-on-camera | Reels + Stories daily, build parasocial connection |
| Faceless + educational | Carousels + text-overlay Reels, no Stories needed |

---

## TL;DR

| Format | Best For |
|--------|----------|
| **Reels** | Reach, discovery, new followers |
| **Carousels** | Saves, shares, education |
| **Stories** | Engagement, loyalty, sales |
| **Posts** | Grid aesthetic, evergreen |

## Part 1: Reels

### What Works in 2025

```markdown
## Reel Formulas

1. **Hook → Value → CTA**
   3 sec hook → 20-50 sec content → CTA

2. **Before/After**
   Show transformation instantly

3. **Tutorial Format**
   "How I [result] in [time]"

4. **Day in the Life**
   Relatable routine content

5. **POV Videos**
   "POV: You're a [role] doing [thing]"

6. **Trending Audio Remix**
   Your niche + trending sound
```

### Reel Specs

```markdown
## Technical

- Aspect: 9:16 (1080x1920)
- Length: 15-30 sec optimal (up to 90 sec)
- Cover: Add custom thumbnail
- Captions: Always add (most watch muted)
- Audio: Trending sounds boost reach
- Text: Add hooks on first frame
```

### Reel Hooks

```markdown
## Opening Lines

"Stop scrolling if you [target audience trait]"
"This changed everything for me"
"POV: [relatable scenario]"
"Why is nobody talking about this?"
"I tested [thing] so you don't have to"
"The difference between [A] and [B]"
"3 things I wish I knew when..."
```

## Part 2: Carousels

### Carousel Structure

```markdown
## 8-10 Slide Formula

Slide 1: Hook (Bold title + promise)
Slide 2: Problem or context
Slides 3-7: Value/steps/tips
Slide 8: Summary or recap
Slide 9: Call-to-action
Slide 10: "Save this" + follow prompt

## Design Tips
- Consistent color scheme
- Large readable text
- One idea per slide
- Use arrows/numbers for flow
- Brand colors in every post
```

### Carousel Ideas

```markdown
## Content Types

1. **Listicle**
   "7 tools every [role] needs"

2. **How-To**
   "Step-by-step guide to [result]"

3. **Comparison**
   "Beginner vs Expert [thing]"

4. **Misconceptions**
   "5 myths about [topic]"

5. **Story**
   "How I went from [A] to [B]"

6. **Resources**
   "My favorite [tools/books/etc]"
```

## Part 3: Stories

### Story Types

```markdown
## Daily Story Ideas

1. **Behind the Scenes**
   Work in progress, real moments

2. **Polls/Questions**
   "Which do you prefer?"
   "Ask me anything"

3. **This or That**
   Engagement driver

4. **Countdown**
   Build hype for launches

5. **Link Sticker**
   Drive traffic (no minimum followers now)

6. **Add Yours**
   Community participation

7. **Slides**
   Mini carousel in Stories
```

### Story Engagement Tactics

```markdown
## Interactive Elements

- **Poll:** 2 options, forces choice
- **Emoji Slider:** Rate something
- **Question Box:** Q&A content source
- **Quiz:** Test knowledge
- **Add Yours:** Start trends

## Best Practices
- Post 3-7 Stories/day
- Mix content types
- Use text on every Story
- Add music
- Close friends for exclusive content
```

## Part 5: Hashtags

### 2025 Hashtag Strategy

```markdown
## Hashtag Mix (15-20 Total)

**Niche (5-7):** Your specific topic
#startuptips #foundertips #saasfounder

**Medium (5-7):** Broader category
#entrepreneur #businesstips #marketing

**Large (3-4):** General discovery
#business #motivation #success

**Trending (1-2):** If relevant
#trending #viral

## Placement
- In caption (after content)
- In comments (alternative, less cluttered)
- First comment within 30 seconds
```

### Hashtag Research

```markdown
## Finding Hashtags

1. Search your keyword → Related tab
2. Check competitor posts
3. Use Instagram's suggestions
4. Rotate sets weekly
5. Track which sets perform best
```

## Part 6: Collab Posts

```markdown
## How to Collab

1. Create post/Reel normally
2. Before posting: Tag → Invite Collaborator
3. They accept, post appears on both profiles
4. Shared engagement, shared reach

## Who to Collab With
- Similar audience size (0.5x-2x yours)
- Complementary niche
- People you've engaged with
- Podcast guests, co-creators
```

## Part 9: Posting Strategy

```markdown
## Best Times (EST)

Peak: 11 AM, 2 PM, 7 PM
Best Days: Tuesday - Friday

## Frequency
- Reels: 3-5/week minimum
- Carousels: 2-3/week
- Stories: Daily (3-7/day)
- Posts: 1-2/week (if doing Reels)

## Content Ratio
- Value: 70%
- Personal: 20%
- Promotional: 10%
```
