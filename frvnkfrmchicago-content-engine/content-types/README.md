# Content Types

Eleven content categories for the Asset Persona content engine. Six core + five AI-vertical subcategories. Each category maps to a color dot and the filter dropdown.

---

## AI_ML

**Color:** Cyan (#00C2FF)
**Description:** AI and machine learning breakthroughs, model releases, benchmark results, research papers, compute infrastructure, and AI product launches. Frank's primary lane.

**Posting Frequency:** Daily

---

## AI_EDUCATION

**Color:** Sky (#38BDF8)
**Description:** AI applications in education -- adaptive learning platforms, AI tutoring, automated grading, curriculum design with AI, EdTech funding, AI literacy programs, and K-12/higher-ed AI policy.

**Posting Frequency:** 2x per week

---

## AI_FINANCE

**Color:** Indigo (#818CF8)
**Description:** AI in financial services -- algorithmic trading, fraud detection, robo-advisors, credit scoring models, regulatory compliance (SEC/FINRA AI rules), fintech AI products, and quantitative analysis.

**Posting Frequency:** 2x per week

---

## AI_HEALTH

**Color:** Pink (#F472B6)
**Description:** AI in healthcare -- diagnostic imaging, drug discovery, clinical trial optimization, FDA AI/ML device approvals, electronic health records, telemedicine AI, and precision medicine.

**Posting Frequency:** 2x per week

---

## AI_PUBLIC_HEALTH

**Color:** Emerald (#34D399)
**Description:** AI in public health -- epidemiological modeling, disease surveillance, social determinants of health analysis, CDC/WHO AI initiatives, health equity analytics, and population health management.

**Posting Frequency:** 1-2x per week

---

## AI_DENTISTRY

**Posting Frequency:** 1-2x per week

---

## DEV_TOOLS

---

## Category Assignment Rules

1. If a headline is about a model or AI capability broadly, it is AI_ML
2. If AI is applied to learning, schools, or students, it is AI_EDUCATION
3. If AI is applied to banking, trading, or financial regulation, it is AI_FINANCE
4. If AI is applied to clinical care, drugs, or medical devices, it is AI_HEALTH
5. If AI is applied to populations, epidemics, or public policy, it is AI_PUBLIC_HEALTH
6. If AI is applied to dentistry, oral health, or dental imaging, it is AI_DENTISTRY
7. If a headline is about a tool engineers use, it is DEV_TOOLS
8. If a headline is about people, jobs, or careers, it is CAREER
9. If a headline is about community, memes, or social dynamics, it is CULTURE
10. If a headline is about creative output or design tools, it is CREATIVE
11. If a headline is about money, companies, or regulation, it is INDUSTRY
12. When in doubt, default to the category Frank would talk about with more passion
