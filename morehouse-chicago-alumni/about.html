About our chapter

Morehouse Men
in Chicago

The Chicago Area Morehouse College Alumni Association brings together graduates of Morehouse College who live and work across the greater Chicago area. For decades our members have carried the Morehouse bond far beyond the campus gates — into medicine, law, finance, technology, education, ministry, and entrepreneurship.

Morehouse College was founded in 1867 in Atlanta, Georgia, and remains one of the nation’s leading institutions for the education of men. Our Chicago chapter is part of the national network of alumni associations that keep that legacy alive, city by city.

Our mission

What we do for Chicago

Four commitments guide the chapter’s work across the region, year after year.

Scholarships

We raise and award scholarships for Chicago-area young men accepted to Morehouse College, easing the path to a Morehouse education.

Mentorship

Our members mentor students and recent graduates, opening doors and sharing the guidance that shaped their own careers.

Recruitment

We help Chicago students discover Morehouse, connecting prospective Men of Morehouse with alumni who know the journey.

Brotherhood

We gather for events all year — networking, service, and celebration — keeping the Morehouse fellowship strong across the city.

The board leads chapter operations, fundraising, recruitment, and community engagement.
