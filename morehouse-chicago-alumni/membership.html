Pay by Zelle or Mail a Check

Zelle and mailed checks are fully accepted and go directly to the chapter's Chase Business account. A board member will confirm and activate your membership once payment is received.

Zelle (Chase Business)
Send Zelle to the chapter's Zelle address (board-provided at provisioning). Use your full name and "dues" in the memo.
Mail a Check
Make check payable to the Chicago Area Morehouse College Alumni Association. Mail to the chapter mailing address (board-provided at provisioning). Write your full name and "dues" on the memo line.
After payment is sent, contact a board member so your account can be confirmed. Standard dues: $75 per year. Premium dues: $150 per year.
