Zelle and mailed checks go directly to the chapter's Chase Business account and are recorded by a board member.

Zelle (Chase Business)

Mail a Check
Make check payable to the Chicago Area Morehouse College Alumni Association. Mail to the chapter mailing address (board-provided at provisioning). Write your full name and donation designation on the memo line.
After sending, please let a board member know so your gift can be recorded and acknowledged.
