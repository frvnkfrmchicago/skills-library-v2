Scholarships

Investing in
Chicago’s future

Each year the Chicago Area Morehouse College Alumni Association awards scholarships to Chicago-area high school seniors who have been accepted to Morehouse College. Recipients are selected on academic achievement, community service, and financial need.

Every dollar given to the Scholarship Fund goes toward tuition, books, and support for these students — helping the next generation of Morehouse Men make the journey from Chicago to Atlanta and back again.

How it works

From application to award

1. Apply

Chicago-area seniors accepted to Morehouse College reach out through our contact page to start an application with the scholarship committee.

2. Review

The board reviews academics, community service, and financial need, and meets each candidate to understand their goals.

3. Award

Scholarships are awarded ahead of the fall term, and recipients join a network of alumni mentors for the years that follow.
